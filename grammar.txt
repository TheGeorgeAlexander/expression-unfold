Grammar
-----------------------------------------------------------
expression    ---> term
                |  term "+" expression
                |  term "-" expression

term          ---> factor
                |  factor "*" term
                |  factor "/" term

factor        ---> NUMBER
                |  "-" NUMBER
                |  IDENTIFIER
                |  function_call
                |  "(" expression ")"

function_call ---> IDENTIFIER "(" argument_list ")"

argument_list ---> expression
                |  expression "," argument_list



Regular Expressions
-----------------------------------------------------------
NUMBER        : [0-9]+(\.[0-9]+)?
IDENTIFIER    : [a-zA-Z_][a-zA-Z0-9_]*
